dimod.responses.TemplateResponse.add_sample¶

TemplateResponse.add_sample(sample, energy, data={})[source]¶

Loads a sample and associated energy into the response.

Parameters:
  • sample (dict) – A sample as would be returned by a discrete model solver. Should be a dict of the form {var: value, …}. energy (float/int): The energy associated with the given sample.
  • data (dict, optional) – A dict containing any additional data about the sample. Default empty.

Notes

Solutions are stored in order of energy, lowest first.

Raises:
  • TypeError – If sample is not a dict.
  • TypeError – If energy is not an int or float.
  • TypeError – If data is not a dict.

Examples

>>> response = TemplateResponse()
>>> response.add_sample({0: -1}, 1)
>>> response.add_sample({0: 1}, -1, data={'n': 1})
