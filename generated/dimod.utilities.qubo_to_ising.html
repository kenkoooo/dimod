dimod.utilities.qubo_to_ising¶

qubo_to_ising(Q)[source]¶

Converts a QUBO problem to an Ising problem.

Map a binary quadratic program x’ * Q * x defined over 0/1 variables to an Ising model defined over -1/+1 variables. We return the h and J defining the Ising model as well as the offset in energy between the two problem formulations, i.e. x’ * Q * x = offset + s’ * J * s + h’ * s. The linear term of the QUBO is contained along the diagonal of Q.

See ising_to_qubo(h, J) for the inverse function.

Parameters:Q – A dict of the QUBO coefficients.
Returns:A dict of the linear coefficients of the Ising problem. A dict of the quadratic coefficients of the Ising problem. The energy offset.
Return type:(dict, dict, float)
